Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CaloShape collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the CaloShape mobile application and related services.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use CaloShape, we may collect or process the following information:

  • Account information, such as email address, sign-in method, profile name, and account identifiers.
  • Profile and goal information, such as age, height, gender, weight goals, nutrition goals, and activity preferences.
  • Food and nutrition information, including food logs, saved foods, calories, macros, micronutrients, meal times, and nutrition history.
  • Images you upload for food recognition, barcode or nutrition-label analysis, or related app features.
  • Progress data, such as weight records, water records, workout records, fasting data, and daily summaries.
  • Health Connect data, if you grant permission, such as steps, active calories burned, exercise sessions, and related history needed for activity and progress features.
  • Subscription and entitlement information needed to verify premium access through Google Play.
  • Technical information, such as device identifiers, app version, locale, network diagnostics, logs, and anti-abuse or security signals.

How We Use Information

  • To provide calorie tracking, food logging, nutrition estimation, weight tracking, workout tracking, and related app features.
  • To personalize nutrition goals, summaries, progress views, reminders, and app settings.
  • To process food photos, labels, barcode data, or other inputs using AI-assisted recognition and nutrition analysis.
  • To verify subscriptions, restore purchases, manage entitlements, and prevent abuse.
  • To send account, verification, support, security, and service-related messages.
  • To improve reliability, diagnose errors, protect the service, and comply with legal obligations.

Health Connect Data

If you choose to connect CaloShape with Health Connect, CaloShape may request permission to read health and fitness data such as steps, active calories burned, exercise sessions, and related history. We use this information to estimate daily activity burn, update your calorie dashboard, and provide workout and progress insights.

Health Connect access is optional. You can grant or revoke access at any time in Android Health Connect settings. CaloShape does not use Health Connect data for advertising. If CaloShape stores activity summaries or derived progress records from Health Connect data, those records are handled under this Privacy Policy and may be deleted through the account deletion process where applicable.

Third-Party Services

CaloShape may use trusted third-party services to operate the app, including:

  • Google Play Billing for subscription purchase and entitlement verification.
  • Google sign-in or authentication services when you choose to sign in with Google.
  • Android Health Connect, if you grant permission, for reading selected health and fitness data from your device.
  • AI and nutrition analysis providers for food recognition and nutrition-label parsing.
  • Email providers for one-time passcodes, support, and service messages.
  • Cloud hosting, database, cache, storage, monitoring, and security providers.

These providers process information only as needed to provide their services to CaloShape.

Health and Nutrition Information

CaloShape is a wellness and nutrition tracking tool. Nutrition estimates may be incomplete or inaccurate. CaloShape is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or professional health advice.

Data Retention

We retain data only for the periods needed to provide CaloShape, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and prevent fraud or abuse. The main retention periods are:

  • Account, profile, food, nutrition, weight, water, workout, fasting, notification, authentication-token, and local-cache data: deleted when an account deletion request is completed. Encrypted backups expire within 90 days.
  • Food-recognition images and related results: automatically deleted after 3 days when a record is pending, failed, or not saved; automatically deleted after 32 days for saved records and history.
  • Google Play entitlement audit records: account identifiers are pseudonymized and the audit record is retained for five calendar years after the terminal subscription event.
  • Encrypted Google Play purchase tokens: retained only for re-verification, acknowledgement, and refund handling, for up to 180 days after the terminal subscription event.
  • Referral reward ledgers and terminal referral claims: the deleted party is pseudonymized and records are retained for five calendar years after the final outcome.
  • Routine referral risk signals: hashed identifiers only, retained for 24 months. Denied referral risk signals: hashed identifiers only, retained for five calendar years.
  • Completed account-deletion requests: account identifiers are pseudonymized and retained for three calendar years for compliance and support audit purposes.

A hash, purchase-token hash, or pseudonym is still personal data, not anonymous data. We restrict access to it and delete it when its retention period ends. A documented, approved, and time-bounded legal hold may delay deletion only where required by law.

Account and Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your CaloShape account and associated app data. For instructions, visit Delete your CaloShape account.

We may retain the limited pseudonymized records described in the Data Retention section for legal, security, fraud-prevention, payment, tax, dispute-resolution, or compliance purposes. Deleting your CaloShape account does not automatically cancel an active Google Play subscription; subscriptions must be managed through Google Play.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. No system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, but we work to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

Children

CaloShape is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to CaloShape, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
